Welcome to the Island of Taniti

Taniti is a small island in the Pacific where you can find various terrian, sandy beaches, a harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and an active volcano. The indigenious population is about 20,000 has recently added tourism to their local economy and wish to become a destination spot.
Here you will find what you need from lodging, transportation, dining, sightseeing, and entertainment. We welcome you to our island and hope you will enjoy your time.
