Entertainment on the Island

Here on the Island on Taniti we have many sources of entertainment. We have many wonderful beaches, hiking trials through the jungle,l a guided tour to the top of the volcano, helicopter tours of the island, snorkling, charted fishing tours, zip-lining, and breath taking sights. If your are more into the nightlife, we have several pubs, a dancing club, movie theatre, arcade centers, and bowling. During the day we have our local museum, art galleries, and a microbrewery.
There is something for everyone here on the island. Come and explore and experience our culture.

We will help you plan your days!
